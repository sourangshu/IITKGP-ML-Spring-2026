Syllabus (Broad Indication)

  • Foundations: Introduction & concept learning, Linear models for classification and regression.
  • Kernels & Trees: SVMs and kernel methods, Decision Trees, Bagging, Random forests.
  • Probabilistic ML: Naive Bayes, Gaussian Mixture Models, Graphical Models.
  • Advanced Ensemble: Boosting and XGBoost.
  • Neural Networks: Architectures, Backpropagation, and Deep Learning basics.
  • Theory & Meta-Learning: Learning Theory, Metrics, Active, Transfer, and Multi-task learning.
  • Ethics: Explainability and Trustworthiness in AI.
  • Unsupervised: Clustering techniques and Latent Variable models.
